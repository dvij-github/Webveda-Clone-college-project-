ABOUT THIS COURSE


        
"Being a writer doesn't pay the bills"

In today’s digital age, this is far from the truth.
        
If you’re passionate and consistent with your writing, it’s time you take it to the next level.
        
Make Writing A Career is designed to empower you with the essential skills and knowledge to make money

doing what you love - writing.

From fundamental concepts to practical insights and strategies, this course will teach you everything you need to know about monetising your writing

  • Understanding the different styles of writing.

  • Identifying your writing niche.

  • Identifying which platform best suits your niche.

  • Creating a system for endless content ideas.

  • Building strong writing habits.

  • Creating a portfolio.

  • Finding your ideal clients.

  • Learning the art of pitching.

  • Learning how to price your services.

  • Finding your ‘why’ : your purpose and motivation.

  • Unveiling the secrets to grow your writing career.

COURSE CURRICULUM

Introspection

What motivates you to write? What should you write about and why?

Go through the journey of finding your Ikigai, the intersection between your passion, strengths, and market demand, and finding your “Why” to create a strong foundation for your writing career.

Creation

Out of all the writing forms out there, which one is the right fit for you?

A search result can tell you all the pros and cons for each writing form, but our activities will help you identify which writing form plays to your strengths. Plus, get access to our system at Brand warikoo to NEVER run out of content ideas.

Specialisation

How do you pick a niche when you have multiple interests and knowledge areas?

Apply our unique framework to find your writing niche, which platform is suited for it, and how you can remain consistent with your writing process.

Monetisation

How do you ACTUALLY begin making money and unlock an income stream from writing?

We will guide you through everything right from showing your work, building visibility, pricing strategies, identifying your clients, pitching to them successfully, and scaling your writing career to make sure it works for you long-term.
